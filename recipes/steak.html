Steak

Description

Restaurant Style Steak

Ingredients

  • 16 oz Ribeye
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat a cast iron pan on medium high
  2. Let the steak come to room temperature and pat with a paper towel to remove any excess moisture
  3. Use a dime sized drop of olive oil and evenly coat the steak with it on all sides
  4. Season the steak generously with salt and pepper on all sides
  5. Once the pan is ripping hot, lay the steak in with no additional oil to sear. Flip the steak after 3 minutes.
  6. Once the steak is flipped, stir the butter, garlic cloves, and thyme into the pan and begin basting the steak
  7. Flip the steak every 2 minutes and continue basting until it reaches an internal temperature of 130F
  8. Let the steak rest for 10 minutes, then enjoy.
