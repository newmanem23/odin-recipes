Mac n Cheese

Description

A classic Ed & Becca dish on weeknights

Ingredients

  • Banza Mac n Cheese - 1 box
  • 2 sausage links
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • Olive oil
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cut sausage into 1/2" discs and sear 2 minutes per side on medium heat
  2. Boil water and cook pasta for 9 minutes
  3. While pasta is cooking, toss broccoli and olive oil into the pan the sausage was cooked in, stirring frequently
  4. After broccoli has some char on some of the sides, throw a dash of water into the pan and cover. This will steam the broccoli
  5. Strain the pasta when al dente
  6. Stir the cooked pasta, milk, and cheese into the pot used for cooking until its incorporated.
  7. Stir in the cooked sausage and broccoli
