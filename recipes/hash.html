Hash

Description

Ed's classic breakfast hash. A real crowd pleaser!

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Pork Belly
  • Green Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium
  2. Cut pork belly into small cubes and sear in the pan until crispy
  3. Remove pork belly from the pan onto a plate with paper towel, leaving the grease in the pan
  4. Add cubed sweet potatoes into the pan, coating in the fat and leaving to get a nice sear on each side
  5. Once the outside of the potatoes have some color, add salt, pepper, cumin, paprika, and garlic powder, and stir to coat
  6. Cover the pan and occasionally stir the potatoes to get an even cook
  7. When potatoes are fork tender, toss in the chopped green onions and cooked pork belly until incorporated, then remove from the pan
  8. Cook 2 sunny side eggs in the remaining pan grease and place them on top of the hash!
